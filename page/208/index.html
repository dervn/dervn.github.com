On the road - 1.12 [摘录]

2007-06-24
日常。笔记

A pain stabbed my heart, as it did every time I saw a girl I loved who was going the opposite direction in this too-big world.

每次看到心仪的女孩和我擦肩而过，消失在这个世界上，从此再不相见，我的心就一阵刺疼。
